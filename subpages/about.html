Hi! Jackie here!

I am a digital arts student at the University of Waterloo Stratford School of Interaction Design and Business

After adapting to the mindset of always think from the users’ perspective, I found the way I see the world completely changed. I started to feel more empathy towards not just the users, but everyone around me. I was able to connect with people better, and as a result, I feel happier. 

I was always into the design, however, I didn't go to an Art school for I felt my art lacks meaning. Unlike most artists, I simply wanted to make things look pretty. I was also into computer programming and psychology. After I learned about UI/UX, it is the first time I feel like I meant it when I say I'm passionate about design. 

The more I learned the field of human-centered design, the more I realized there are just so much more to learn. I'm really grateful that I get to do what I love every day and see myself improving.

My goal is to be able to create products that bring joy to people and make people feel more connected with each other.
