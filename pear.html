About Us

We aim to create a platform called PEAR that pairs students with charities, to provide students with opportunities to work on real life problems that charities are facing. PEAR would essentially be a project based job searching site where students apply for the projects posted by charities. (Sample projects: developing a website, conducting a survey, writing a research paper for cancer research centre, etc.) Charities are also able to host a hackathon.
